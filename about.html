About this App

This app is designed to excite users to the power of STEM topics in general, and aviation in particular. It contains pictures, videos, and text that clearly portray the basics of the science behind flight. The topics covered include lift, propellor propulsion, and jet propulsion. A short quiz is available as well so the user can test his or her knowledge.
